Before You Reach Out quick answers

What information should I include?

Share what you're trying to accomplish, your timeline, and budget range. The more context, the better I can help determine if we're a good fit.

How quickly will you respond?

I aim to respond within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters, please mention it in your message subject or description.

What's the smallest engagement you take?

The smallest project is a discovery audit ($3k-5k, 1 week). This gives you clear recommendations and scoped projects—no obligation to move forward. I don't do hourly consulting or free strategy calls.

What's your current availability?

I'm currently accepting new projects. Timelines vary based on scope—let's discuss what works for your schedule.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. I work with clients worldwide and handle different time zones regularly. Location doesn't matter—good work and clear communication do.

What happens after I send a message?

I'll review your project details and respond within 24 hours with honest feedback on whether I'm a good fit, initial questions, and next steps if we move forward.
