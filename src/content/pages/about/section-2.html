My Story journey & philosophy

My journey into growth engineering began over 15 years ago when I discovered the intersection of marketing, product, and technical execution. What started as WordPress development evolved into building scalable revenue engines for high-growth companies.

Based in Austin, Texas, I've had the privilege of working with companies like Shopify, DoorDash, Shipt, and Crunchbase—building demand generation systems, e-commerce platforms, and data-driven growth strategies.

I'm deliberately bad for business—my business, anyway. When I solve your problem correctly, you stop needing me. That's terrible for recurring revenue and the only honest way to work.

My Approach

Foundation-Obsessed

Zero-to-One Specialist
