Frequently Asked common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-4 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with different time zones. Most communication happens via email, video calls, and project management tools.

What's your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% upon completion for smaller projects. Larger projects are broken into milestone-based payments for better cash flow management.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All projects include a warranty period for bug fixes. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages for updates, security monitoring, and continued optimization.

Can you work with my existing team?

Definitely! I collaborate seamlessly with internal teams, agencies, and other freelancers. I can adapt to your existing workflows and communication preferences.

What if I need revisions?

All projects include a set number of revisions. I believe in getting it right, so we'll work together until you're completely satisfied with the final result.
