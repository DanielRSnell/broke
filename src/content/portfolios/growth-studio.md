## Project Overview

Growth Studio is a premium agency portfolio template showcasing the BROKE.dev brand identity. Designed for multi-disciplinary digital agencies, it balances creative excellence with data-driven growth strategies. This template positions the agency as a full-service partner capable of handling everything from brand strategy to technical implementation.

## Key Features

### Hero & Brand Presence
- Bold typography with the BROKE brand identity
- Dynamic gradient backgrounds and modern aesthetics
- Clear value proposition above the fold
- Strategic CTA placement for lead generation
- Trust signals (years of experience, projects completed, client satisfaction)

### Service Offerings Display

**Comprehensive Service Grid**
- Full-Stack Development
- Growth Marketing & SEO
- Brand Strategy & Identity
- UI/UX Design
- WordPress & CMS Solutions
- Custom Application Development

Each service includes:
- Icon representation
- Brief description
- Related case studies
- Starting investment range

### Client Portfolio Section

**Case Study Showcase**
Interactive project cards featuring:
- Client logo and industry
- Project category (development, marketing, design)
- Key metrics and outcomes
- Visual preview (mockups, screenshots)
- Technology stack used
- Project timeline

**Filterable Grid**
Visitors can filter by:
- Service type
- Industry
- Project size
- Technology used

### Team & Process

**Meet the Team**
- Team member profiles with specializations
- Distributed team locations
- Culture and values
- Career opportunities link

**Our Process**
Visual timeline showing agency workflow:
1. Discovery & Strategy
2. Design & Planning
3. Development & Implementation
4. Launch & Optimization
5. Growth & Iteration

### Social Proof Elements
- Client testimonials with photos
- Brand logos of companies worked with
- Industry awards and recognition
- Press mentions
- Client retention rate

## Technical Stack

- **CMS**: WordPress with custom theme
- **Framework**: Timber/Twig templating
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4 with custom design system
- **Animation**: GSAP or Framer Motion
- **Forms**: Gravity Forms or WPForms
- **SEO**: Yoast or RankMath
- **Performance**: WP Rocket, ShortPixel
- **Icons**: Lucide icon library
- **Fonts**: Custom web fonts (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans)
